Mitchell Sandwich

A Perfect Sandwich

Sandwiches are so underated. I love a sandwich, there easy, fullfilling and they can be amazing on their own or with a packet of crisps. This is a perfect breakfast sandwich with extra flavours to make it even more delicous. I will share with you my secrets.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread
  • 1 Avacado
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2 rashes of Smoked Bacon
  • Garlic Infused Olive Oil
  • Chilli flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mature Cheddar Cheese
  • Balsamic Glazwe

The How To

  1. Get that grill on - medium heat
  2. Spank that bread.
  3. Then slice that sexy ass bread to however thicc you want it baby
  4. Get the Bacon in
  5. Toast your slices (we dont want it to crispy, just slightly taosted so we have a nice soft middle).
  6. Olive oil in a pan. Crack open the egg. (IMPORTANT - We want the yellow bit runny so dont over cook)
  7. Once Sourdough is tosted, smosh the avoacdo into once side of it
  8. Drizzle Garlic Infused Olivi Oil onto the Smoshed Avacado
  9. Sprinkle Salt, Pepper and Chilli flakes onto the smoshed Avacado
  10. Add the cooked Egg
  11. Add the Bacon
  12. Now add the Cheeeeeeeese. (if your timing is right, the Cheese will melt on the Bacon)
  13. Drizzle a little amont of the Balsami Glaze onto the inside of the other toasted bit of Sourdough
  14. Slap it on top. Push down slighty on top of the sandwich to really seal it all in.
  15. ENJOY!
