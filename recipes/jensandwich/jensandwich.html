The Jen Sandwich

Thanking my Mum Again

This Sandwich comes from my late teens/early 20's. I was at Drama 5 days a week, working or at the gym straight after and I was getting home around 23:00 everynight. Too late to cook a nice decent meal, too fussy to put crap like pot noddles into my body. My mum knew the answer and would often makes this bad boy just as I would arrive home. It became a favourite in the household. When friends came over, they would inquire to see if said sandwich was on the menu/availale for the night. Thanks Mum.

Ingredients

  • Brie Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Cranberry Jam
  • Pannini

The How Too

  1. Get your sefl a Jen - if she is unavailbe, your on your own abd should follwo the steps below.
  2. Turn on Sandwich Grilling Machine (I used the George Foreman LEAN MEAN FAT REDCUING MACHINE)
  3. Get frying or Griling your bacon (2-3 rashes) in your frying pan or oven grill.
  4. Cut Pannini in half lenght wise
  5. Spread a nice layer of Cranberry jam across the bottom half.
  6. Once bacon is cooked, lay bacon on the bottom half of the Pannini
  7. Slice Brie Generously and place ontop of cooked bacon
  8. Close Pannini, place of Sandwich Grill, close and let the magic happen (between 1-3 mins)
  9. If you done it right, the Brie should be melted and slightly over spillin.
  10. ENJOY another banging sandwich!
